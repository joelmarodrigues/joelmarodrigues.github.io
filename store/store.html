The Rubik's Cube

In the midst of the stress and pressure of high school, I stumbled upon a colorful, cube-shaped antidote: the Rubik's Cube. As I twisted and turned the cube, the weight of exams and deadlines began to melt away. But soon, what started as a simple distraction became a full-blown addiction as I pushed myself to solve the cube faster and faster.
